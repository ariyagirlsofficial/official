About Us

Our Story

ARiYA Official is a K-pop and J-pop music group created in Brazil by Eric Oliveira. The group was originally composed of five members, but after some challenges, there were changes in the lineup! Currently, the group consists of three members: Nari Jung (정나리), Miyu Takahashi (高橋 美優), and Haeun Lee (이해은).

ARiYA Official released their first album, "Starting Over," on September 10, 2024, available on digital platforms. The album's name reflects a fresh start after changes in the group's formation. We are exploring a bolder style and experimenting with genres such as Electro-Pop, Future Bass, and Dance/Electronica. We're just getting started, stay tuned!
